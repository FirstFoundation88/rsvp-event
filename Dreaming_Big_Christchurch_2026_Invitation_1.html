About the Event

A day designed
for wāhine who dream big

Dreaming Big is a full-day event that brings together young New Zealand women with an inspiring group of senior businesswomen, leaders, and changemakers — all in one room, ready to share their stories.

Hosted at ANZ Christchurch, this is a rare opportunity to hear real career journeys, ask the questions you've always wanted to ask, and connect with women who have navigated challenges, taken risks, and built extraordinary lives.

You've been personally invited by First Foundation because we believe you have the potential, the curiosity, and the drive to make the most of a day like this.

On the Day

What to expect

01

Talks & Q&As with Senior Business Leaders

Hear from some of New Zealand's most accomplished businesswomen. Ask the questions that matter to you in an open, relaxed Q&A format.

02

Entrepreneurship — Real Journeys, Real Stories

Three New Zealand entrepreneurs share how they built their ventures, what they wish they'd known, and what it really takes to back yourself.

03

Financial Wellbeing Session

Practical, honest conversation about money — building financial confidence and independence from the ground up.

04

Inspiring Young Women Leaders Panel

A discussion with three young New Zealand women who are already making their mark — and not that much older than you.

05

Personal Brand & the Online World

How to navigate the digital space with confidence, stay safe online, and start building a personal brand that opens doors.

06

Ask Anything — Open Floor

A rare session where you can ask the entire OnBeingBold panel anything you like. No question is too big or too small.
